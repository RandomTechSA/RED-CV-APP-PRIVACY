# REDCV - Privacy Policy & App Information

## App Overview
RED CV is a comprehensive Android application featuring document scanning, OCR text recognition, PDF generation, notepad functionality, CV/resume checking, and premium features through in-app billing.

## Data Collection

### Document Processing
- **Document Scanning:** Images processed locally on device
- **OCR Processing:** Text recognition performed locally using ML Kit
- **PDF Generation:** Documents converted to PDF locally
- **Notepad:** Notes stored locally on device only
- **CV Analysis:** Resume processing done locally, no cloud uploads

### Billing & Financial Data
- **Payment Processing:** All transactions handled through Google Play Billing
- **Purchase History:** Stored locally for receipt validation only
- **Payment Information:** No credit card or sensitive financial data stored in app
- **Subscription Management:** Handled entirely through Google Play services
- **Purchase Tokens:** Required for receipt validation with Google Play

### Analytics & Performance
- **Crash Reports:** Anonymous crash data sent to improve app stability
- **Usage Analytics:** Anonymous usage patterns for feature optimization
- **Device ID:** Collected for fraud prevention (Google Play requirement)

## Data Storage
- All user documents and notes stored locally on device
- No user content transmitted to external servers
- Purchase receipts stored locally for validation
- No third-party analytics beyond basic crash reporting

## Permissions Required

### Essential Permissions
- **Camera:** Required for document scanning functionality
- **Storage:** Required for saving PDFs, documents, and notes
- **Internet:** Required for billing validation and app updates

### Billing Permissions
- **Billing:** Required for in-app purchases and premium features

## Data Sharing & Third Parties

### Google Play Services
- **Google Play Billing:** Payment processing and subscription management
- **Google ML Kit:** Local OCR processing (no data leaves device)
- **Google Analytics:** Anonymous crash reports and basic usage statistics

### Data Sharing Policy
- We do not share user documents or personal content with third parties
- No user data sold to advertisers or data brokers
- No user profiling for targeted advertising
- No sharing of purchase history beyond Google Play requirements

## Security Measures
- Local encryption for sensitive documents
- Secure keystore for app signing and billing validation
- SSL/TLS encryption for any network communications
- No network transmissions of user document content
- Secure receipt validation through Google Play

## User Rights & Control
- **Full Control:** Complete control over all your documents and data
- **Data Deletion:** Ability to delete all app data at any time
- **Data Export:** Export your documents and notes anytime
- **Purchase Management:** Manage subscriptions through Google Play
- **Opt-out:** Disable analytics in app settings

## Billing & Purchases
- **Digital Goods:** Premium features unlocked through in-app purchases
- **Subscription Model:** Monthly/yearly subscription options available
- **One-time Purchases:** Individual feature upgrades available
- **Refund Policy:** Follows Google Play Store refund guidelines
- **Receipt Validation:** Secure validation through Google Play services

## Children's Privacy

- No features that would appeal to children specifically

## International Data Transfers

- No international data transfers of user content
